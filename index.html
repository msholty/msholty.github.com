About Me

Web development. It gets me up in the morning. It can sometimes put me to sleep at night (if you've done it, you know what I'm talking about). I am passionate about technology to the point where it's not only my career, but it's a part of me.

I crave the chance to be constantly challenged, and thrive in environments where I can use my creativity to its max. I don't want to be given just a task, but a problem to solve.

I have worked in a multitude of professional environments, and at this point I know I have the ability to assimilate to my surroundings well. I've worked with the waterfall methodology and agile methodology on small teams and big teams. I've worked with people on a global scale. I love meeting new people and getting to know them better. I guess you could say I'm a people person.
